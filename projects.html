Einzel Lens

Electromagnetic Lening allows researchers to see into the nanoscale world and up to 10,000,000x magnificaiton, compared to regular optical lens used in regular microscopes which can only reach up to 2000x magnificaiton. Electromagnetic lensing uses electrons, after passing them through electric fields causes the electrons to change their trajectory, which results in the electrons converging to a focus point, allowing an image to be formed.
